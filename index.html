    How to read this

    The black line is where the road user has already been.

    The green line is what really happened after that moment.

    The colored lines are the model's three guesses about what might happen next.

    The percentages show how strongly the model believes each guess, and the risk card summarizes how uncertain or behaviorally sensitive the scene looks.
